This website is a list of issues of the Michigan Register for downloading. This website does not look for words inside the text of any documents. The size of each PDF file can exceed 30 MB. For word searches inside of issues please consult the HathiTrust page for this title. Click on the Timeline link in the navigation bar above to quickly identify an issue by year. This site was created for quick lookups by library staff assisting researchers looking for specific issues of the Michigan Register. For subject matter questions please contact the Library of Michigan, Law Library.
